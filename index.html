// What We Do

Our Services

We focus on getting your energy situation right — not just selling you a system.

🔍 01
Solar System Audits

Already have solar? We audit existing installations to find underperformance, wiring faults, or inefficiencies — and give you a clear report of what's working and what isn't.

🔋 02
Battery Backup Solutions

Tired of load shedding? We design and recommend the right battery backup system for your needs and budget — so the lights stay on when Eskom says otherwise.

📡 03
Maintenance & Monitoring

Keep your system producing at its best. We offer ongoing monitoring, performance reviews, and scheduled maintenance to protect your investment long-term.

How It Works

01
Request a Free Consultation

Fill out our quick quote form or give us a call. Tell us about your property, current setup, and energy goals — no obligation, no jargon.

02
On-Site Assessment

We visit your property to assess your energy usage, roof orientation, existing wiring, and any existing solar equipment. Real data, not guesswork.

03
Clear Recommendation Report
