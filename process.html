How It Works

Clean in 4 Simple Steps

From first contact to restored efficiency — no surprises, no hidden costs.

01

Request a Quote

Submit your details via the contact form, WhatsApp, or phone. We'll confirm panel count, roof access, and service area within a few hours.

02

Pre-Clean Reading

On arrival, we record your system's current output to establish a baseline — giving your clean measurable proof of performance improvement.

03

Professional Clean

Soft-brush deionised wash, frame wipe-down, and junction box visual inspection. Conducted to manufacturer guidelines — your warranty stays intact.

04

Post-Clean Report

A service report with before/after readings, any flagged issues, and recommendations for ongoing maintenance — emailed to you same day.
