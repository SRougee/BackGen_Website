Business Overview
BackGen provides professional solar panel cleaning and maintenance across Gauteng, South Africa. The service restores peak performance, improves efficiency, extends system lifespan, and maximises solar ROI.

Service Area
- Boksburg, Germiston, Alberton & surrounding East Rand areas (Gauteng)

Key Messaging
- Dirty panels can lose up to ~30% efficiency.
- Manufacturer-approved, warranty-safe techniques using deionised water and soft-brush methods.
- Pre- and post-clean efficiency readings; service reports emailed same day.

Services
1) Professional Solar Panel Cleaning
   - Deionised water and soft-brush techniques
   - Pre/post efficiency readings
   - Safe for all panel brands and coatings
   - Roof-mounted and ground-mounted systems
   - Maintenance packages available (quarterly / bi-annual)

2) Efficiency Audit
   - Inspects underperforming strings, shading, dirty panels, inverter inefficiencies
   - Provides a written report and actionable recommendations

3) Battery Health Check
   - State-of-charge testing
   - Cell balancing verification
   - Thermal inspection for lithium and lead-acid battery banks

4) SSEG Registration
   - Helps navigate Small Scale Embedded Generation process
   - Handles paperwork and follow-up

How It Works (Process)
1) Request a Quote
2) Pre-Clean Reading (baseline)
3) Professional Clean (soft-brush deionised wash, frame wipe-down, junction box visual inspection)
4) Post-Clean Report (before/after readings and recommendations)

Clients / Testimonials (summary)
- Clients report measurable output improvements after cleaning and recommend quarterly contracts.
- SSEG registration support resolved delays and achieved municipal approval.
- Clients highlight professional communication and written pre/post reporting.
